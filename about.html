HISTORY OF URS TAYTAY

The University of Rizal System Taytay Campus was established in 2005 and first resided at Rosario Ocampo Elementary School. The first offered courses were Civil Technology, Bachelor of Secondary Education major in English and General Education, Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurial Management, Call Center Training Program, Twoyear Civil Technology, and Two-year Garments Technology. During the first batch of students, there were six pioneer instructors who guided and mentored them.

In 2007, URS Taytay was transferred to its new building at the Rizal Technopark and offered the two-year course practical nursing program. In the same year, the campus had its first Campus Dean in the person of Prof. Grenelita D.C. Bilbao, and its first chancellor, Dr. Reneecilia B. Paz De Leon.

In 2013, the campus offered BSE English, BEED, BS Psychology, and BS Information Technology due to public demand.

Today, URS Taytay has emerged as the leading provider of health care with Nursing as its flagship program. The URS Taytay community continuously commits to deliver excellent and quality services in order to meet the demands of the globally competitive world.
